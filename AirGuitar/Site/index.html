    About Us

    Our Team

    We’re a pair of fun loving, guitar smashing entrepreneurs bred from the flames of Singapore Management University School of Information Systems.

    CEO/Founder

    Kim Evangelista

    Started AirGuitar because effects pedals are too damn expensive. His hair is much longer than what the picture above shows. Strongly believes that beer is good for idea generation and coffee is for execution.

    CFO

    Brandon Seet

    Joined AirGuitar after a glass of cold beer with Kim, after that he sold his soul to making awesome music & products for Air Guitar. You'll mostly find him in our well stocked pantry, munching away on a peanut butter jello sandwich.



    Our Culture

    We strive to maintain the open culture often associated with startups, in which everyone is a hands-on contributor and feels comfortable sharing ideas and opinions. In our weekly all-hands (“TGIF”) meetings—not to mention over email or in the cafe—AirGs ask questions directly to Kim or Brandon about any number of company issues. Our offices and cafes are designed to encourage interactions between AirGs within and across teams, and to spark conversation about work as well as play.
